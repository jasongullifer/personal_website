Interests

Below are a few of my non-school-related interests. I realize I didn't get this page up for a while and I swear its not because I'm boring!

  • Cooking

    If, for some reason, I ever give up on psycholinguistics, I'd probably train to become a chef.

    Cooking is great! Not only can you make whatever you're in the mood for, but if you're feeling adventurous you can try something you've never had before. All it takes is a trip to the grocery store.

    Ashley and I try to cook together almost every night. I think you tend to eat healthier foods if you aren't ordering out all the time. Plus, once you get good you can make better hot-wings!

    Another reason I like cooking? Probably because it leads to another of my favorite activities: eating!

    Baking is fun too!

  • Computers

    Tinkering with computers is another favorite pastime of mine. I recently ventured into the world of Linux, and I'm happy to say I'm not coming back (to Windows that is).

    I feel like every Linux user should have a "last straw" event that cemented their decision to switch. Mine was when a fresh install of Windows XP recognized none of my hardware. I tried installing Ubuntu and in 20 minutes (as opposed to about an hour for Windows) I was good to go. And people say that Linux doesn't recognize hardware!

  • Outdoor Things

    Although I'm a computer geek, I do enjoy being outside (although I don't tan.) I love going to the swimming at beaches and lakes. I could stay in the water all day. Whenever I'm back home, I make it a priority to go to the beach.

    I also like to go for the occasional hike in the woods. I'm looking forward to finding some places to hike around here this summer.

    A friend of mine built a camp up in the boonies of Maine with his family. His father's a carpenter, so they did it all by hand over the weekends. I tried to help out whenever I had the time. Initially it was just a plot of land where we would camp out. Now that land has an amazing house on it.
